Rice Krispies

Delicious Rice-Krispies from the comfort of your house!

By buying this ingredients and following these steps, beautiful girls named Sofia Jimenez Cruz will be able to make the most delicious rice-krispies at home with their very handsome boyfriends named Ivan Berezin!

*it might be easier to just buy rice krispies*

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 5 cups crispy rice cereal

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat; add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.
  3. Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13-inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper. Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
